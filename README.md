# Credit-Card-Financial-Dashboard
- Analyzed $56.5M in revenue across 10,000+ customers using a custom Power BI data model
- Found income to be the strongest driver of revenue (0.94 correlation) — far more 
  than card tier, job, or education
- Discovered the Blue (entry-level) card makes up 91% of customers but only 83% of 
  revenue, while Platinum customers spend nearly 3x more per person
- Identified the 40–50 age group as the single biggest revenue segment, contributing 44%
- Traced week-over-week revenue swings to shifts in customer income mix rather than 
  actual spending behavior changes, and projected revenue to stay flat around $1.05M/week
